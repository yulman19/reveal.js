Joséphine Spritz

  • 2oz. Xeres
  • 1/2oz. simple syrup
  • 1/2oz. fresh lemon juice
  • 2oz. rosé wine (grenache/syrah)
  • 2pc. strawberries
  • 2dsh. rubarb bitters

METHOD

SHAKEN

Add all ingredients into a shaker, shake into an ice filled old-fashioned glass.

GLASS

Highball / wineglass

GARNISH

Salicornia + Strawberry
